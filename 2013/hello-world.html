Hello World

2013-02-15 by czardoz

For want of something to do during the holidays, I decided to start this blog. This is just the mandatory 'Hello World' post. Much more is yet to come. I won't stick to any one subject with this blog. Any and everything on my mind will be posted here! I'll also try to be much less narcissistic here, than on my website ;-) I don't want to make this an actual blog, with long essays and posts. I'll use it as a mini-blog. Plus it's much easier to write stuff here, I really don't want to install Wordpress or some other heavy framework.

Update This post was written back when I had a separate blog and a website. I now only have a blog.
